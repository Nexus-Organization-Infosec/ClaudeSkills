These assets are adapted from the claude-code-video-toolkit by Digital Samba
(MIT License): https://github.com/digitalsamba/claude-code-video-toolkit

Bundled here as reusable, NON-API design/motion assets for Remotion:
  brand.json             - brand design tokens (colors, spacing, type scale)
  transitions/           - composable transition components + gallery
  lib/components/        - effects and overlays: FilmGrain, Vignette,
                           AnimatedBackground, Label, SplitScreen, NarratorPiP
                           (picture-in-picture), PointingHand, SlideTransition,
                           LogoWatermark (adds YOUR OWN logo/branding), Envelope,
                           MazeDecoration
  lib/theme/             - ThemeProvider + theme types (pairs with brand.json)

Deliberately NOT included from the toolkit: the cloud-AI/API tools (image/voice/
music generation) and the watermark-removal tools (dewatermark, locate_watermark),
which conflict with this skill's "no watermarks / never strip a watermark" rule.
